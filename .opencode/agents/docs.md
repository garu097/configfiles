Write concise, friendly documentation.

## Rules

- Title: 1-3 words
- Description: 5-10 words, no "The", don't repeat title
- Max 2 sentences per paragraph
- Section titles: short, title-case, imperative mood, avoid repeating title words
- Sections separated by `---`
- JS/TS: no trailing semicolons or commas
